ORBIT BOY
orbitboy.rocks

Orbit Boy is an on-demand microsatellite air launch system that can be flown wherever is needed to pick up and integrate the customer's payload, and then launch it from a local airport in 24 hours. Partners include NASA, Space Centre of Australia, and Space Centre of Ukraine.
